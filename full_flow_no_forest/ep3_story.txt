Title: Meeting the Sky Painter

Scene: Rainbow Princess meets a wise, elderly cloud artist named Cirrus, who is sadly looking at his empty palette and brushes, surrounded by canvases of dulled sky scenes. They are in a whimsical studio built into a large, fluffy cloud.

Story: Rainbow Princess sought out Cirrus, the legendary Sky Painter, hoping he held clues about the disappearing colors. She found him in his studio, looking quite dejected with his brushes dry and his palette strangely empty. Rainbow Princess felt a surge of determination; together, they would surely uncover the mystery of the fading sky.
