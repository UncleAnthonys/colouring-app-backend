Title: A Fading Glow

Story: One sunny morning, Sparkle noticed that the usually vibrant magical lights in her village square were beginning to fade, making everything look a little dull. She remembered stories of the ancient Whispering Crystal, the source of all magic, and wondered if it might be losing its sparkle. With a brave heart, Sparkle decided she had to investigate what was happening and find a way to help her beloved village.

Scene: Sparkle standing in a colorful village square, looking up at a dimly glowing magical lantern, a worried expression on her face. Small, whimsical houses line the background, their usual sparkle dulled.
