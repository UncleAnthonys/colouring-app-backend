Title: The Glimmering Grotto's Secret

Scene: Rainbow Princess carefully steps into a dimly lit grotto within a floating mountain, her newly created small, bright rainbow illuminating ancient carvings on the cave walls. Intricate symbols glow gently under the rainbow's light.

Story: In the Glimmering Grotto, an old legend spoke of hidden wisdom only revealed by true light, but it was too dark to see anything. Rainbow Princess conjured a focused, narrow rainbow that gently lit the grotto walls, causing ancient symbols to shimmer and reveal a long-forgotten star map. Her unique light solved the mystery!
