Title: The Murky Mist

Story: A strange, creeping grey mist began to seep into Cloudia, stealthily absorbing all the colors it touched, leaving behind only dullness. Rainbow Princess, feeling a surge of brave determination, tried to repel the mist with her own innate radiant glow, but its power seemed overwhelming. She realized this peculiar mist was not merely a symptom, but the very core of the mystery she needed to unravel.
