Title: A Soft Awakening

Scene: Green Monster is sitting on a mossy log in a slightly dim forest glade, a tiny sprout reaching towards the soft green glow emanating from its body. Small, friendly forest creatures peek from behind trees.

Story: One morning, Green Monster noticed a faint, calming glow around its pastel green body. It felt like a warm, gentle hug, and the forest creatures seemed more peaceful than usual.
