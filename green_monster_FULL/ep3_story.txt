Title: Brightening the Gloomy Cave

Scene: Green Monster walks confidently into a dark, slightly damp cave, its radiant green light pushing back the shadows and revealing shimmering crystals. A tiny, scared cave creature peeks out from behind a rock.

Story: A group of lost fireflies couldn't find their way out of a dark cave, feeling very scared. Green Monster bravely entered, its body shining like a gentle lantern, leading the way with a comforting light.
