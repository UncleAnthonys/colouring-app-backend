Title: A Message from the Prism Sprite

Scene: Rainbow Princess holds a glowing, miniature rainbow in her hands. A tiny, translucent sprite, shimmering with faint colors, floats before her, pointing towards a dark mountain in the distance.

Story: A tiny, shimmering Prism Sprite, usually bursting with light, appeared before Rainbow Princess with a worried message. The Sprite explained that the mythical Color Crystal, hidden deep within the Whispering Peaks, was dimming, causing all the world's colors to fade. Rainbow Princess knew she had to journey to the peaks and restore the Crystal's glow.
