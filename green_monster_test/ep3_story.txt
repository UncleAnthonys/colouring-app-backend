Title: The Muddy Riverbank Clue

Scene: Green Monster, covered in a thin layer of light brown mud that barely hides its green, looks surprised as it peeks over a muddy riverbank. A small, strange, colorful pebble is embedded in the mud nearby, glowing faintly.

Story: Following faint tracks, Green Monster found itself at the muddy riverbank, where the green-brown muck helped it blend even more! Suddenly, a tiny, glowing pebble caught its attention, shimmering with an unfamiliar light. Could this unusual rock be connected to the mystery of the dulling glow and wilting flowers?
