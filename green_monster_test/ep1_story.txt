Title: The Whispering Woods Mystery

Scene: Green Monster, almost invisible against a backdrop of deep green leaves and ferns, peeks from behind a large tree, holding a magnifying glass and looking curious. A tiny, rare sparkly bug is just visible on a nearby leaf.

Story: In the Whispering Woods, a curious case unfolded: tiny, sparkling insects had stopped glowing at night! Green Monster, with its amazing green color, knew it could blend in and observe without being noticed. It quietly slipped into the trees, ready to uncover the mystery.
