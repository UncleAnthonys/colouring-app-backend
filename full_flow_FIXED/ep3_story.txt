Title: Sculpting with Sunshine Yellow

Scene: Rainbow Princess carefully shapes a shimmering yellow statue of a giggling sun with her glowing hands, surrounded by a crowd of delighted onlookers in a sunny plaza. The air around her shimmers with golden light.

Story: The children had cheered loudly when she created the temporary bridge, inspiring Rainbow Princess to bring more beauty to the town square. She used her sunshine yellow light to sculpt a magnificent, shimmering statue that captured the joyful spirit of the day. Everyone watched in wonder as the colorful creation seemed to glow with its own cheerful warmth.
