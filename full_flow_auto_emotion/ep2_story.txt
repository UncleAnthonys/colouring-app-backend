Title: The Whispering Wind's Hint

Story: As Sparkle wondered how to help, a gentle breeze rustled through the colorless trees, carrying a faint, melodic whisper. It spoke of a mischievous cloud creature, the 'Color Collector,' who loved to keep all the bright shades for itself. Sparkle's five eyes glimmered with curiosity, eager to learn more about this peculiar problem.
